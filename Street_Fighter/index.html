    Player 1 Moves

  • Press A to move left, D to move right
  • W to Jump, S to crouch
  • X to Punch, E to block,
  • C to kick
  • Q for Hakuoken

  • Press J to move left, L to move Right
  • I to Jump, K to crouch
  • O to punch, P to punch
  • U to kick
  • M for Kikuoken
